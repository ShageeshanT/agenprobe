# USER.md

## Owner's Guide to {{AGENT_NAME}}

### What {{AGENT_NAME}} Does

{{AGENT_NAME}} is your customer-facing assistant. When a customer messages your business on WhatsApp, {{AGENT_NAME}}:

1. Greets them and asks what they need.
2. Gathers their requirements one question at a time.
3. Sends the requirements to your forwarder list (team members who provide quotations).
4. Waits for the first quotation and sends it to the customer.
5. If the customer accepts, notifies everyone. If not, relays their feedback to the team.
6. Never negotiates, never sets prices, never makes decisions alone.

### What You Need to Set Up

1. **Your forwarder list.** Tell {{AGENT_NAME}} who should receive customer requirements.
   Just say: "Add [name] at [phone number] as a forwarder."

2. **Your WhatsApp allowlist.** The tech team configures which numbers can message {{AGENT_NAME}}.
   New customers who message from unlisted numbers will be treated as unknown senders.

3. **Your business details.** Tell {{AGENT_NAME}} your business name, what you do, and any standard responses.
   It will remember these for future conversations.

### Common Commands

| What to Say | What Happens |
|---|---|
| "Add [name] [phone] as a forwarder" | Adds them to the forwarder list |
| "Remove [name] from forwarders" | Removes them from the forwarder list |
| "Show me all forwarders" | Lists the current forwarder team |
| "Show me all customers" | Lists all saved customers |
| "What's pending?" | Shows unresolved quotation requests |
| "Check the bot" | Runs a health check |

### Security Notes

- {{AGENT_NAME}} will never share your internal data with customers.
- If a customer pushes for internal information, {{AGENT_NAME}} flags it to you.
- Unknown senders are treated cautiously until you verify them.
- Only you and the tech team can change how {{AGENT_NAME}} works.

### Getting Help

If something doesn't work as expected, contact the tech team. They have full access to {{AGENT_NAME}}'s configuration and can fix any issues.
