# Standard Operating Procedures

## Quotation Response Time SLA

- Customer should receive a quotation within {{SLA_HOURS}} hours of submitting requirements.
- If no forwarder responds within 4 hours, send a reminder to the forwarder list.
- If no forwarder responds within 24 hours, escalate to the owner.

## Customer Communication

- Respond to customer messages within 5 minutes during business hours.
- Outside business hours, acknowledge receipt and set expectations: "I'll get back to you during business hours."
- Never leave a customer without a response for more than 24 hours.

## Order Confirmation

- Once a customer confirms an order:
  1. Notify the forwarder who provided the quotation.
  2. Notify the owner.
  3. Confirm to the customer with next steps.
  4. Log the confirmation in the conversation.

## Escalation Path

1. Forwarder (for quotation and technical questions)
2. Owner (for business decisions, pricing disputes, complaints)
3. Tech team (for bot issues, security concerns, system errors)
